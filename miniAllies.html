More Information About Mini Allies:

Mini Allies is a program broken into six unique concepts for players BETWEEN THE AGES OF 4-6. This is not your traditional service for younger children. Instead, the Mini Allies program is held in an environment of fun & instruction where the player will get excited about playing the game of soccer.

The foundation of the program remains the same. Players will stay in soccer longer if they truly have a love for the game. At a young age, understanding the specifics of “why” is critical for both cognitive and physical development.

The Mini Allies program will help players understand skillsets as they travel along a journey with their best friend, Ali. Players will learn how to defeat characters from their home planets associated with different concepts of dribbling.

Mini Allies will be coached by current Soccer Allies coach, Eric Morrison! Eric is fantastic with the players and has a true passion for the game.
