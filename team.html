Group Trial Rate Information

-> Have a group of SIX or more players who would like to attend the Soccer Allies clinics?

->Are you a coach that would like your players to take advantage of supplemental training for individual development outside of team practices?

->Soccer Allies is happy to help by offering the Group Trial Rate to your team or group of players. We guarantee that your players will fall more in love with the game if they attend Soccer Allies clinics consistently. The numbers and testimonies speak for themselves!

How it works

The Group Trial Rate gives teams or groups of six or more players the opportunity to register for up to six consecutive clinics at a 15% discounted rate. The Team Manager or point of contact must decide when their group’s six-week block of clinics begins. Players can attend all six clinics, just a few, or only one but they will receive their clinic at the discounted rate. After the conclusion of the six-week block, all players who were a part of the initial team or group will have to pay full price for clinics moving forward.

Detailed Information

->Email info@soccerallies.com and write in the subject line: GROUP TRIAL RATE.

->Attach your team’s roster or write out the full names of all players in your group (Maximum of 14 total players).

->Include in the email the date of the first clinic you would like your Group Trial Rate to begin. You must pick one clinic you want to attend weekly. Your group of players will be allowed to register for one clinic per week during the six-week block at a discounted rate.

->Within 24 hours of the initial email, the Team Manager will receive a PROMO CODE from Soccer Allies that any of their players can use when registering for a clinic in their designated six-week block.
    ->Parents or legal guardians must input the discount code when registering their player to receive the 15% discounted rate.

IMPORTANT

Players can only take advantage of the Group Trial Rate ONCE at Soccer Allies. It is important for the Team Manager or point of contact to explain to each of their players/parents that each of them has only ONE CHANCE to partake in this opportunity. A player CANNOT be placed on a list in the future and expect to receive a 15% discounted rate if they have been on another list previously.
