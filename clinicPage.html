How the Next Level Challenge Works...

The Next Level Challenge is a unique soccer training program designed to help encourage players from the U8-U12 age groups and gauge their success through individual development. The format is structured in twelve levels. Each level is designed to build off the last and crafted to have a challenging but attainable goal to overcome.
Every level will have each of the following: ball work, a specific concept, and a challenge. The ball work is simply to help the players become more comfortable with the ball at their feet. The concept will test their knowledge and understanding of why, when, and how certain touches can be used to beat a defender. Lastly, the challenge is designed for the players to prove they truly understand the concept at each level by using the touches and skills against a defender in game-like situations. Once all three have been perfected, the players will be rewarded by moving to the next level. A major emphasis is put on the players grasping the concept at each level. All graduations are at the full discretion of the Soccer Allies
