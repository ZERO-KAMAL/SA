Camps

Camp Description

  • -The players will be split into groups based off of birth year or desired level of play.
  • -Each day will have its own concept (Soccer Allies Dribbling Concepts, Defensive Third, Middle Third, and Finishing).
  • -Coaches will rotate groups every day so that the players get a feel of different coaching styles.
  • -There will be a specific challenge each day that players will have the opportunity to perfect and win a prize.
  • -Coaches will elect a Camp MVP for each group which will be based on Character, Leadership Qualities, and Level of Play.
